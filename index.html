Introduction

Waste accumulation is a primary global concern, rising rapidly with population growth and industrialization.

  • Global Impact: Municipal solid waste (MSW) expected to reach 3.40 billion tonnes annually by 2050.
  • Hospitality Sector: A major contributor to waste generation, facing challenges in resource management and sustainability.
  • Current Issues: Improper disposal, carbon emissions, and lack of efficient routing systems.

Resource Management Challenges

Hospitality industries struggle with waste management, customer retention, and adherence to SDG goals.

Inefficiency

Manual collection leads to overflow and health hazards.

Sustainability

Lack of "Green" practices affects eco-conscious guest retention.

Technology Gap

Limited use of IoT for real-time monitoring and routing.

Aim & Objectives

Aim

To develop a web-based system incubating Smart Waste Bins across hospitality industries that tracks waste levels and connects to garbage collection services.

Key Objectives

  • Design a web-based eco-tech architecture.
  • Integrate Garbage Collection Services (GCS) & Smart Bins.
  • Implement secure payment gateways & resource management.
  • Evaluate usability using the Technology Acceptance Model (TAM).
